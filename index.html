TDEE Calculator

'TDEE'or Total Daily Energy Expenditure is the total amount of calories you burn in a day. Your TDEE is determined by adding your BMR to the total amount of calories you burn through your daily activities!

FFMI Calculator

'FFMI'or Fat Free Mass Index is a measure of how much muscle you are carrying for your height and weight. A higher FFMI means you are carrying more muscle. The average male scores around 19 and the average female around 15. How do you stack up?
